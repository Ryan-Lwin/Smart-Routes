Smart Routes

Smart Routes is a highly-designed tech company, centered in Silicon Valley, USA, partnered by Apple to produce the best possible solutions to resolve the problem of traffic congestion that is impeding traffic flow. This is an advanced corporation that regards the this acute issue with expertise and close inspections in order to make the right decisions for what we are going to implement. As for now, the Smart Routes is on course of a large-scale project to induce a constructive transportation system that will enhance the way of travelling in our contemporary society and ease the pressure of traffic congestion on commuters, particularly during the rush hour.

Our Vision

Smart Routes simplifies city living by using advanced technology to improve traffic flow and create more accessible urban environments. At Smart Routes, we're dedicated to transforming urban mobility through innovative technology. Our vision is a future where city streets are dynamic and efficient, catering seamlessly for commuters and communities. With a focus on excellence and sustainability, we leverage AI and data-driven insights to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve quality of life worldwide. We aim for more than convenience; we envision a fundamental shift in urban transportation, making every journey smoother, safer, and eco-friendly. Through relentless innovation, we're committed to shaping a smarter, more connected future.

Outline of Our Project

We have a far-reaching ambition to expand our project to an enormous extent. Our main goal is to smoothen the flow of traffic and transportation in cities. We have conceived two main solutions to traffic congestion issues. One of such intervention we propose is installing Smart Traffic Lights that have similar functions as the normal lights, but in company of AI cameras, switches and detection software. Another solution is promoting pedestrian detection to diminish the disturbance of pedestrian transportation and enhance municipal mobility.
